What We Do

Optimize store performance and profitability through real-time video data analytic

The retail industry is on a constant and profound transformation.

Retail video analytics has gone beyond the traditional domain of security and loss prevention by providing retailers with insightful and actionable business intelligence such as store traffic statistics and queue data in real time. Such information allows for enhanced customer experience, optimized store performance, reduced operational costs, and ultimately higher profitability.

Using the same security infrastructure in the store, video analytics can now provide fact based answers in real time.

How many people visited my store today/this week/this month? (People Count)

How many people who visited my store actually made a purchase? (Conversion Rate from No. of Transactions)

How many customers visited a specific promotional stand / gondola end vs. the total sales of the promotion for a specific day/week/month?

Which spots in my store have the greatest traffic activity (Hot Zones) and where do customers spend their time in the store (Dwell Time)?

How many people are standing in the queue at a specific time or interval and for how long? (Queue Time)

Why is conversion rate, hot zone, dwell time and queue time data important for retailers?

The conversion rate is an indicator of high importance to retailers since it tells how well a store is performing. The traffic statistics can also be used by store managers to make merchandising or staff planning decisions.

Knowing where customers go and stay within a store is often as important as counting them. Hot zone and dwell time data enables store managers to optimize store layout for better product placement. In addition, this data can be used to evaluate or enhance the effectiveness of sale or advertising displays.

Nobody enjoys a queue. At best, we regard them as a necessary evil. At worst, they can be the difference between a sale and an abandoned shopping basket (with customer dissatisfaction guaranteed). The analysis of queue time data can provide an actionable solution that predicts how many cashiers should be active to prevent queues. By predefining threshold/s you can set triggers such as speaker announcements and staff prompts to improve customer service.

What We Offer

RetailFlux is the technology-leading provider of retail analytic solutions in the market. It offers the latest innovations in people counting, staff exclusion, heat mapping, queue management, and shopper flow. RetailFlux solutions are supported by a secure cloud-based business intelligence platform that leverages best-in-class artificial intelligence technology to analyze collected data. RetailFlux technology is used by large organizations around the world in sectors ranging from retail, banks, transportation facilities, and public buildings.

With RetailFlux, you are able to monitor your shoppers and get summaries of their actions and activities including passersby, impression, and dwell. You are able to see just how many people went inside your store, what sections of your store they linger or dwell the most, where they stopped, and son. Camera views from different angles enable you to generate shopper flow, dwell time, and impression events.

The software uses the information it captures to provide you with easily understandable graphical visualizations that indicate your shoppers’ movement. This functionality helps you to quickly recognize a shopper’s journey, including the minute details. You also get a clear view of the queueing areas and all the related indicators and data like the number of entries to queue area, the average service time of your staff, abandonment rate, and more.
